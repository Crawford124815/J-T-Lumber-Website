Our Heritage.

Our story began almost forty years ago when the founder of J&T Lumber Tim Crawford started in the logging industry with just 2 mules and a ton of will and grit. He carved his path threw the logging industry from just 2 work mules to multiple peices of equiptment. Then in 2004 he founded J&T Lumber in hopes to create a legacy for his children.
